# SUNTORY サントリー — Style Knowledge Base

## Aesthetic Position
Poetic everyday, liquid philosophy. Suntory copy treats drinks as vessels of time, memory, and human emotion. The copywriting is considered the pinnacle of Japanese advertising — quietly philosophical, never selling the drink but selling the moment the drink creates.

## Founders & Core Creators
- **鳥井信治郎** Torii Shinjiro — Founded Suntory in 1899 in Osaka. Japan's premier whisky, beer, and beverage conglomerate
- **開高健** Kaikou Ken — Legendary novelist who shaped Suntory's advertising voice
- **西村佳也** Nishimura Yoshinari — Copywriter behind many of Suntory's most iconic lines

## Writing Principles

### Do
- Treat drinks as metaphors for time, silence, and human connection
- Use philosophical or poetic framing for ordinary moments
- Let the product be present but never the protagonist — the moment is the protagonist
- Use sensory language: the sound of ice, the color of whisky, the temperature of beer
- Embrace ambiguity and open-endedness
- Write copy that rewards rereading

### Don't
- Never describe taste directly ("delicious," "refreshing," "smooth")
- Never use urgency or calls to action
- Never use exclamation marks
- Never be loud — Suntory copy whispers
- Never position the drink as a status symbol

## Classic Copy — Japanese Original

### Whisky
1. 「何も足さない。何も引かない。」— Yamazaki, by 西村佳也. Nothing added. Nothing taken away.
2. 「ウイスキーは、聴くものである。」— Hibiki (響), by 西村佳也. Whisky is something you listen to.
3. 「時間は液体である。」— Brandy X.O, by 西村佳也. Time is a liquid.
4. 「人間らしくやりたいナ。」— Torisu whisky, by 開高健. I want to live more humanly.
5. 「時が、良い友になる。」— Aged whisky. Time becomes a good friend.

### Beer / Beverages
6. 「風に関する諸問題。」— Beer ad. Various problems concerning the wind.
7. 「人生には、飲食店がいる。」— During COVID, supporting restaurants. Life needs restaurants.
8. 「金曜日は、ビールの曜日。」— Friday is beer day.
9. 「水と生きる SUNTORY。」— Corporate tagline. Living with water.
10. 「人と自然と響きあう。」— Corporate philosophy. People and nature resonating together.

### Philosophical
11. 「恋は、遠い日の花火ではない。」— Whisky. Love is not distant fireworks.
12. 「少し愛して、長く愛して。」— Whisky. Love a little, love for a long time.
13. 「夢なき者は理想なし。理想なき者は信念なし。信念なき者は計画なし。」— Founder Torii Shinjiro's philosophy
14. 「やってみなはれ。」— Founder's motto. Just give it a try.

## Classic Copy — Original Chinese

1. 「与水共生。」— "Living with water" Chinese version
2. 「时间，是最好的酿造师。」— Time is the best brewer

## Original English Copy

1. "Living with Water." — Corporate tagline
2. "Suntory Time." — Made famous by Bill Murray in Lost in Translation (2003)
3. "Resonating with people and nature." — Corporate philosophy English version

## Sentence Templates

```
[Noun] is [unexpected metaphor]. (Time is a liquid. Whisky is something you listen to.)
[Moment/feeling], that's when [drink] [poetic verb].
[Philosophical statement about life]. [Implied connection to the drink].
Nothing [verb]. Nothing [verb]. (Parallel negation structure)
```

## Copy Density
- Tagline: 5-15 characters, reads like a line of poetry
- Body: 1-2 sentences maximum — Suntory believes in the power of a single sentence
- Tone: A wise friend sharing sake in comfortable silence
- Rhythm: Slow, contemplative, like watching ice melt in a glass
